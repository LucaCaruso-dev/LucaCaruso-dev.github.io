LOGISIM

Logisim is a simple and easy to use digital circuit simulator. It is designed for educational purposes and allows users to create and simulate digital circuits using a graphical interface.

Logisim is a free and open-source software, which means that anyone can use, modify, and distribute it without any restrictions. It is available for Windows, Mac OS X, and Linux operating systems.

Logisim is a powerful tool for learning about digital circuits and computer architecture. It provides a wide range of components, including logic gates, flip-flops, multiplexers, and more. Users can create complex circuits by connecting these components together and simulating their behavior.
